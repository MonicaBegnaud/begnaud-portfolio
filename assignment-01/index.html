Top 5 Thanksgiving Sides

  1. Mashed Potatoes

    Mashed potatoes are my personal favorite. I prefer them with garlic and too much butter. My dad makes them with little pieces of green apple and onion mixed in.

  2. Green Bean Casserole

    The green bean casserole has somehow become my assigned task. My family swears I make it better even though I just follow the recipe on the back of the crispy onions.

  3. Bread/Rolls

    My children will be very upset that bread is not the first item on the list. Every year it is a competition to see who can eat the most rolls.

  4. Sweet Potato Casserole

    It is an ongoing debate on whether or not you put marshmallows or crushed nuts on top of the casserole. My vote is for marshmallows every time.

  5. Stuffing

    My husband just considers stuffing wet bread and is not a fan, but I think it is an important staple to the overall meal.
